About Us

Trusted Logistics and Warehousing Service Provider

JBN Express Ltd is a well known and established name in the Courier and Cargo industry worldwide. JBN started itself way back in the year 2020 with a vision to offer complete Logistic and courier services to corporate and many other business establishments catering to diverse fields. JBN believes that in today’s competitive business environment every second counts and time is money. Understanding this, JBN has strived to maintain its service standards at the optimum. In a short span of 3 years, JBN on the background of its superior quality service rapidly tied up with many corporate and semi – corporate Organizations for their International & Domestic Courier & Cargo requirements. JBN continuously strives at being recognized as the best logistic and courier company in Kenya. It intends to achieve this by maintaining a high quality service every time. Our service gives you the power and flexibility to effectively manage all your business. It gives you full control over transportation decisions and related costs.
